Sample Demonstration Overview

This page showcases our Cheap2Chic system using one representative device pair and five music genres. The low-end earphone is IE-L1: Fransun E20, and the target high-end earphone is IE-H2: Sennheiser IE 300.

Because most visitors do not have these exact devices, all audio here is recorded after playback through the corresponding earphones. Concretely, each table column means:

  • Low-End: original track played through the low-end earphone, then recorded.
  • Cheap2Chic: audio enhanced by our system, played through the same low-end earphone, then recorded.
  • High-End: original track played through the high-end earphone, then recorded.

This setup lets you perceive the qualitative improvements delivered by Cheap2Chic under realistic playback conditions.
